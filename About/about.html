About Us

Our project is dedicated to ensuring the safety of elderly individuals through an advanced fall detection system. Using state-of-the-art sensors and AI algorithms, our gadget can detect falls in real-time and send immediate alerts to caregivers or emergency contacts. We believe in making technology accessible and lifesaving for everyone.

Our system integrates seamlessly with smartphones, smartwatches, and home automation devices to provide a comprehensive safety net. The lightweight and wearable gadget ensures comfort while maintaining high accuracy in detecting falls. Our mission is to offer peace of mind to families and independence to the elderly.
